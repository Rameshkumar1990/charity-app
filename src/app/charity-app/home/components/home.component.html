Our Goal

There are hundreds of NGOs that are working towards a better nation, a better tomorrow, a better future. We not are much different. Our only goal is to support the people who needs us directly. We want our donations to reach those who need directly and completely, which is why we do not like to spend much on operational costs. We interact with the people directly, understand their needs and post their details open for contributions. We strive not just to fulfill someone’s desires but to empower them to self sustain and achieve their desires through their own potential.

We work together to bring a smile on everyone’ face everyday.

- Pavani (Founder, WeGive Organisation)
